Welcome to MASHER!

MASHER is a web app created with Vanilla JavaScript and the Web Audio API that showcases one of music's underappreciated genres: the mash-up. Combine your favorite pop songs in real time by either 1) choosing an instrumental from a popular song and layering other songs' melodies over it or 2) choosing a popular song melody and layering other songs' instrumentals underneath.

Watch as the pictures on the left and right panels of the screen shift with your selections and change in time with the beat of the song!

To hear a single part by itself, select "Instrumental Only" or "Vocals Only". To return to hearing both parts at once, select "All".

Some fun combinations of vocals and instrumentals to try:
Ariana Grande (instrumental) + Justin Bieber (vocal)
Lizzo (instrumental) + Charli XCX (vocal)
Robyn (instrumental) + Katy Perry (vocal)
Grimes (instrumental) + Kim Petras (vocal)
