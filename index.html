Welcome to MASHER!

MASHER is a pop song mash-up creator built using Vanilla Javascript and the Web Audio API. Mix and match vocals and instrumentals for your enjoyment from contemporary pop songs from artists such as Ariana Grande and Lizzo. To just hear the vocal or instrumental part, select the "Solo instrumental" or "Solo vocals" buttons respectively.

Some of my favorite combos to try:

- Ariana Grande (instrumental) + Justin Bieber (vocal)
- Lizzo (instrumental) + Charli XCX (vocal)
- Robyn (instrumental) + Katy Perry (vocal)
- Grimes (instrumental) + Kim Petras (vocal)
