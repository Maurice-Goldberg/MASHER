Welcome to MASHER!

MASHER allows you to mash together your favorite pop songs into a pop music frankenstein! Either choose a single song instrumental and hear other songs' vocals parts over it, or choose a single vocal part and hear other songs' instrumentals underneath, creating bangin' pop song mash-ups in real time!

The pictures on the left and right panels of the screen toggle with your selections and change in time with the beat of the song.

To hear a single part by itself, select "Instrumental Only" or "Vocals Only. To return to hearing both parts at once, select "All".

Some fun combinations of vocals and instrumentals to try:
- Ariana Grande (instrumental) + Justin Bieber (vocal)
- Lizzo (instrumental) + Charli XCX (vocal)
- Robyn (instrumental) + Katy Perry (vocal)
- Grimes (instrumental) + Kim Petras (vocal)
